Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What is job marketing?+

Job marketing is a strategic, proactive approach to career advancement. Rather than passive job searching, we actively market your professional profile — applying to targeted roles daily with optimized résumés, recruiter outreach, and data-driven targeting to maximize interview conversion.

Do you guarantee placement?+

While we cannot guarantee placement as hiring decisions involve multiple factors, our structured approach, daily high-volume applications, and optimization significantly increase your chances. Our success-fee model means we're financially invested in your placement — our incentives are fully aligned with yours.

How many applications per day?+

It depends on your track: Prime includes 30 targeted applications daily, Prime Plus doubles that to 60 per day, and Ultra Prime includes comprehensive, white-glove application support tailored to your specific needs and target roles.

Do you help with LinkedIn optimization?+

Absolutely. We provide a complete LinkedIn transformation including headline rewriting, summary enhancement, keyword optimization, skills strategy, and engagement coaching — all designed to increase your visibility to recruiters and drive inbound opportunities.

Is each résumé customized for every application?+

Yes. We tailor your résumé for each application, aligning keywords and phrasing with the specific job description's requirements. This maximizes your chances of passing ATS screening and standing out to hiring managers.

Can freshers and entry-level candidates apply?+

Absolutely. We work with candidates at all experience levels — from fresh graduates to senior professionals. For freshers, we focus on building strong profiles, gaining relevant certifications, and positioning you effectively for entry-level opportunities.

What exactly is the success fee?+

The success fee is 11% of your first year's annual CTC, charged only after you receive and accept an offer. This model aligns our interests with yours — we only earn when you succeed, ensuring our full commitment to your placement.

Do you provide interview coaching?+

Yes. We offer comprehensive interview preparation including mock interview sessions, common question coaching, technical round preparation, behavioral question strategy, and tips for clearing assessment rounds with confidence.

What is the difference between Apply Only and Placement Tracks?+

Apply Only campaigns are flat-rate — we apply to jobs on your behalf for a fixed period (30 or 60 days) with no success fee. Placement Tracks are comprehensive packages that include résumé optimization, LinkedIn overhaul, daily applications, interview support, and a dedicated strategist, with an 11% success fee charged only after you get placed.

What job portals and platforms do you apply on?+

We apply across all major platforms including LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Naukri, Monster, Dice, ZipRecruiter, and company career pages directly. We also leverage recruiter networks and referral channels for wider reach.

How soon can I expect interview calls after signing up?+

Most clients begin receiving interview callbacks within 2–3 weeks of starting their placement track. The timeline varies based on your experience level, target roles, and market demand, but our optimized approach significantly shortens the typical job search cycle.

Do you work with international candidates or only India-based?+

We serve candidates globally. Our strongest placement networks are in India, the United States, and Middle East markets, but we support professionals targeting roles in any geography.

Can I upgrade from one track to another mid-way?+

Yes, you can upgrade your track at any point. For example, if you start with Prime and want to switch to Prime Plus for faster results, we'll adjust your plan and billing accordingly. Reach out to your strategist or our support team to arrange an upgrade.

What is included in the Resume Studio add-on?+

Resume Studio is a one-time $100 service where we build or completely revamp your résumé from scratch. It includes ATS-friendly formatting, role-specific keyword optimization, clean professional layout, and is suitable for freshers, career switchers, and experienced professionals alike.

How does Career Coaching work?+

Career Coaching is a $50/hour 1:1 session with a career specialist. Sessions are 60 minutes and can cover interview preparation, career strategy, salary negotiation, profile review, or any career-related guidance tailored to your goals. You can book as many sessions as you need.

What happens if I don't get placed?+

For Placement Tracks (Prime, Prime Plus, Ultra Prime), there is no success fee if you don't get placed — you only pay the upfront track fee. For Apply Only campaigns, you pay the flat rate regardless of outcome. We are committed to your success and continuously optimize our strategy throughout the engagement.

Do you help with H1B or visa-sponsored roles?+

Yes, we can target visa-sponsored roles as part of your job marketing strategy. We'll filter and apply to companies that are known to sponsor work visas. However, visa approval is subject to employer and government processes beyond our control.

How do I get started?+

Simply click "Get Started" or "Book Free Consultation" on this page, or reach out to us on WhatsApp at +91 82336 33213. We'll schedule a free 30-minute call to understand your goals, recommend the right track, and get you onboarded within 24 hours.
